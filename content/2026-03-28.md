## Quick Win — Apply This Today

**Set up a `/loop` scheduled task for your current engagement.**

The new `/loop` command (released March 2026) turns Claude Code into a background worker. Try this right now:

```
/loop 30m "Check the status of our data pipeline and summarize any errors in PIPELINE_STATUS.md"
```

This runs every 30 minutes and keeps a living status document updated. Perfect for monitoring long-running data jobs, watching for CI failures, or tracking deployment status during a release. It works on all paid Claude plans and requires zero setup beyond typing the command.
